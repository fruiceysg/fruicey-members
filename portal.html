Terms & Conditions *

1. Membership Tiers

New members are registered as a Member. Tiers upgrade immediately when the spending threshold is met within the current 2-month period. The cut-off date resets on the date of upgrade.

2. Tier Benefits & Discounts

Gold (5% off), Platinum (10% off), Diamond (15% off). Discounts are not applicable in conjunction with promotional items or other ongoing promotions.

3. Diamond Tier Perks

Diamond members receive special pricing on new Fruicey product launches and earn double points during their birthday month to accelerate tier progression.

4. Non-Transferability of Points

Memberships are strictly personal and cannot be transferred to another individual. Points reset at the beginning of each 2-month renewal period.

5. Use of Member Benefits

Member benefits are solely for the use of the registered member upon self-collection or delivery. Benefits are not transferable under any circumstances. Members must maintain tier spending levels to continue enjoying tier-specific benefits.

6. Birthday Month Redemption

Birthday month rewards must be redeemed within the member's birthday month. Unused rewards cannot be carried forward to subsequent months. Members must present a valid photo identification (e.g. NRIC, passport, or driver's licence) for identity verification at the time of redemption.

7. Changes to Terms & Conditions

Fruicey reserves the right to amend, update, or modify these Terms & Conditions at any time without prior notice. Continued use of membership benefits constitutes acceptance of the revised Terms & Conditions.
