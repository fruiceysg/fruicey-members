Terms & Conditions

How to earn a stamp

Purchase any regular-priced product in store. One stamp is issued per transaction by Fruicey staff via unique QR scan. Not valid with promotions, discounts, or online orders. In-store purchases only.

1. Rewards Pass

Each stamp is earned from a regular-priced product purchased in store. Not valid with promotions or discounts.

2. No Minimum Spend

No minimum spend required. One stamp per transaction.

3. In-Store Only

In-store purchases only. Online orders do not qualify.

4. Staff-Issued Stamps Only

Stamps are added by Fruicey staff only via unique QR scan. Customers cannot add stamps themselves.

5. Reward

Upon completing 9 stamps, you are entitled to 1 wheel spin prize.

6. Redemption

Prize must be redeemed in store. Not transferable or exchangeable for cash.

7. Card Reset

After redemption, your rewards pass resets to 0 and a new card begins.

8. Non-Transferability

Stamp card accounts are strictly personal and non-transferable. Stamps are strictly not transferable between accounts or customers under any circumstances.

9. Prize Redemption

Wheel prizes must be redeemed in store upon redemption or making payment. Screenshot images are strictly not accepted for redemption. Un-redeemed prizes can be traced back in store by our crew.

10. Amendments

Fruicey reserves the right to amend these terms at any time.
